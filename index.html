"WE PRIDE OURSELVES IN TOP QUALITY CRAFTMANSHIP"

With over a decade of building experience, Derek has had the pleasure of working on homes along the North Coast of Kwa Zulu Natal, ranging from Durban North and La Lucia to Golf estates such as Mount Edgecombe and Zimbali.
Specialising in decking and carpentry,, provide refurbishment and maintenance on existing decks and the building of custom decks to suit the client's requirement, in a choice of timber or composite materials.
The range of decks includes;


  • Pool decks
  • Sun decks
  • Suspended Decks
  • Koi pond decks
  • Water feature decks
  • pathways

Derek's attention to detail and team involvement, both within his team of staff and the professional team appointed to the project, ensures the outcome of a great deck and a satisfied client.

Other specialisations include custom built pergolas, window cladding, boundary fences, patio furniture, headboards, side tables and benches.
