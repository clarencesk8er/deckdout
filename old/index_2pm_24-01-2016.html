ABOUT US

With over a decade of building experience, Mount Edgecombe based, Derek Liebenberg has had the pleasure of building homes along the North Coast of Kwa-Zulu-Natal, ranging from Durban North and La Lucia to Golf estates such as Mount Edgecombe and Zimbali, to name a few.
Specialising in decking and carpentry,, provide refurbishment and maintenance on existing decks and the building of custom decks to suit the client's requirement, in a choice of timber or composite materials.
The range of decks Derek and his team build include pool decks,sun decks,suspended decks,Koi pond decks,water feature decks and pathways.
